Currently a Research Intern at Meta FAIR (Paris).

I'm a PhD student at the [NYU Courant Institute](https://cims.nyu.edu/) in the [Center for Data Science](https://cds.nyu.edu/), advised by [Julia Kempe](https://cims.nyu.edu/~kempe/). I am interested in lots of things, but I currently work on the intersection of AI and Math, including AI for Math (autoformalization, reinforcement learning and LLMs), and Math for AI (information theory, world models, and self-supervised learning).

Previously, I completed a joint BS/MS in Applied Mathematics and Mathematics at UCLA (Departmental Scholars Program), where I was interested in diffusion models, optimal transport, and statistical mechanics. I've spent time as a research intern at ETH Zürich ([Swiss Data Science Center](https://datascience.ch/)) and WorldQuant. 

I'm always happy to chat about research.
